Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

What We Collect

When you request an analysis, we collect:

  • Email address — to deliver your report and communicate about your order
  • YouTube channel URL — to perform the analysis
  • Payment information — processed and stored by Lemon Squeezy (our payment processor). We do not store your credit card details.

What We Analyze

We analyze only publicly available YouTube data: video titles, descriptions, view counts, like counts, comment counts, publish dates, and video durations. We do not access private YouTube Analytics data and never ask for your YouTube login credentials.

How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver the channel analysis report you purchased
  • To communicate about your order (delivery, follow-up questions)
  • To improve our methodology using anonymized, aggregated patterns (never individual channel data)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing (their privacy policy)
  • YouTube Data API — public data retrieval (Google's privacy policy)

Data Retention

We retain your email and channel URL for the duration of our business relationship plus 12 months. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tubedissector.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not use analytics tools on our marketing pages.

Children

TubeDissector is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
