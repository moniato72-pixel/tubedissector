Finding #1 — Title formula

Videos that name specific tools + include a buying decision outperform by 12x

"ChatGPT Plus vs. Free — is it worth $20?" (119K views) vs. "When to Use AI (And When Not To)" (under 2K views). The winning pattern: [Tool A] vs [Tool B] + price anchor + year. The losing pattern: vague, abstract, no tension.

Finding #2 — Revenue left on the table

$340/month in affiliate revenue sitting uncollected

Compares $20/month tools without affiliate links. At 42K views/month with 2% CTR and 5% conversion at 20% commission — that's a second revenue stream with zero extra content.

Finding #3 — Format mismatch

Only mega-hit (344K views) is a Short — but Shorts are less than 5% of output

The channel's single Short generated more views than the next 40 videos combined. Shifting 30% of output to Shorts would multiply discovery without increasing production effort.
